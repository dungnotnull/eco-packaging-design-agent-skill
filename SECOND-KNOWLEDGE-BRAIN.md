# SECOND-KNOWLEDGE-BRAIN — Eco-friendly Packaging Design

## Core Concepts & Frameworks
- **Circular economy (Ellen MacArthur Foundation)** — eliminate waste, circulate materials, regenerate nature; 9R hierarchy (Refuse, Rethink, Reduce, Reuse, Repair, Refurbish, Remanufacture, Repurpose, Recycle, Recover).
- **LCA (ISO 14040/14044)** — goal/scope, inventory (LCI), impact assessment (LCIA: GWP, water, energy), interpretation; cradle-to-gate vs. cradle-to-grave.
- **Cradle to Cradle Certified** — material health, product circularity, clean air/climate, water/soil, social fairness.
- **Design for Recycling** — APR Design Guide (US), CEFLEX (flexible films, EU); mono-material > multilayer; avoid problematic additives (carbon black, PVC, PETG in PET stream).
- **Compostability** — EN 13432 / ASTM D6400 (industrial); home-compost certs differ; requires actual facility access.
- **Regulation** — EU Packaging & Packaging Waste Regulation (PPWR): recyclability, recycled content, reuse targets; EPR fees.
